About Us

Welcome to RATTP, the RATP's official website.
We are a team of two students from Efrei Paris, a French engineering school.
We created this website as part of a project for our web development course.
Our goal is to provide a fun and educational experience for RATP users and metro enthusiasts.
We hope you enjoy playing our game and learning more about the Paris metro system.
